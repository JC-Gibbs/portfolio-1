OUR HISTORY

I fell in love with chihuahuas more than two decades ago. Since, I began breeding I have tried to create healthy, happy, dogs that come as close to the breed standard as possible. Responsible breeders insure quality, pure-bred lines for generations to come.

First and foremost, our dogs are our beloved companions and they are raised inside with lots of love and attention. All pure-bred dogs were bred for a purpose and the Chihuahua was made for nothing but cuddling! KA-Lynn's Chihuahuas is state licensed and inspected by both the state and AKC ensuring that our breeding practices are safe and responsible. All of our available puppies have been thoroughly examined and come with a health record. Additionally, we have a stringent vaccination protocol and our puppies come with all age appropriate vaccinations, de-worming, and flea/tick prevention. We encourage anyone interested in adding a Chihuahua to their family to research the breed and make sure that it is the right fit for your family.
